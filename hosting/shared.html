FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is shared hosting?
Shared hosting means your website shares a physical server with other customers. Resources like CPU, RAM, and disk space are distributed among accounts. It's the most affordable entry point and is ideal for personal websites, blogs, and small businesses that don't require dedicated resources.
Can I host multiple websites on one plan?
Yes — our Business and Pro plans allow you to host an unlimited number of websites under a single account. The Starter plan supports 1 website only. If you need to host multiple sites, we recommend upgrading to the Business plan.
Is WordPress included with my hosting plan?
Yes. All shared hosting plans include Softaculous, which allows you to install WordPress (and 400+ other apps) with a single click. The installation takes less than 60 seconds and requires no technical knowledge.
Do you offer free website migration?
Yes, we offer free website migration for all new customers. Our expert team will move your website, databases, emails, and configurations from your current host to Orbit Cloud with zero downtime. Contact our support team after signing up to schedule your migration.
What happens when I need more resources?
You can upgrade your shared hosting plan at any time from within your control panel. If your site outgrows shared hosting entirely, we make it easy to migrate to a VPS or Cloud hosting plan. Our support team can assist with any upgrade or migration at no extra charge.
How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?
If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of signing up, simply contact our support team and request a refund. We'll process it in full with no questions asked. Domain registration fees are non-refundable but all hosting fees are fully covered.
