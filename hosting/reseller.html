FAQ

Reseller Hosting — Common Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to start a hosting business?
No. Our reseller packages are designed for non-technical entrepreneurs. WHMCS automates billing and provisioning. WHM provides a straightforward interface for managing client accounts. Our reseller support team is available 24/7 to assist with any technical questions.
Will my clients know they are hosted on OrbitHost infrastructure?
No — full white-label branding means your clients only see your brand. You can customise cPanel themes, nameservers, error pages, and support contact details. OrbitHost is invisible to your end customers.
How many client accounts can I create?
All OrbitHost reseller plans support unlimited client hosting accounts with no per-account charge. You can subdivide your disk space and bandwidth across as many accounts as you choose using WHM's resource allocation tools.
Is WHMCS included with reseller hosting?
Yes. All reseller plans include a full WHMCS license. WHMCS handles client account creation, billing, invoicing, payment collection, and support tickets automatically — letting your hosting business operate largely on autopilot.
Can I set my own pricing for client plans?
Yes. You have complete freedom to set your own pricing. OrbitHost charges you a fixed wholesale price and you decide what to charge your clients. Many resellers charge 3–10× the wholesale rate while remaining competitively priced in their local markets.
