FAQ

VPS Hosting — Common Questions

What is VPS hosting and do I need it?
VPS (Virtual Private Server) hosting gives you a dedicated portion of a physical server with guaranteed CPU, RAM, and storage. You need VPS hosting if your website has outgrown shared hosting, you need root access, you run custom applications, or you need consistent performance without resource contention from other users.
Is VPS hosting managed or unmanaged?
We offer both unmanaged and fully managed VPS options. Unmanaged VPS gives you full control and root access to manage your server yourself. Managed VPS includes OS updates, security patching, performance optimisation, and 24/7 monitoring by our team. Ask our sales team about adding managed services to your VPS plan.
Can I install a control panel like cPanel on my VPS?
Yes. You can install cPanel/WHM, Plesk, DirectAdmin, or any other control panel on your VPS. Our Business and Pro plans include a cPanel license at no extra charge. Alternatively, free panels like CyberPanel or HestiaCP are available for self-installation.
How quickly is my VPS provisioned?
VPS servers are provisioned automatically within 60–90 seconds of payment confirmation. You will receive your server credentials and login details via email immediately after provisioning. Our automated setup system operates 24/7, including weekends and public holidays.
What operating systems are supported?
We support Ubuntu (20.04, 22.04, 24.04), Debian (11, 12), CentOS 7, AlmaLinux 8/9, Rocky Linux 8/9, Fedora, and Windows Server 2019/2022. Custom ISO uploads are available on request for advanced users.
