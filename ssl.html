Why SSL is Non-Negotiable

SEO Ranking Boost

Google uses HTTPS as a ranking signal. Websites with valid SSL certificates rank higher in search results than unsecured HTTP sites.

Customer Trust

The padlock icon and HTTPS prefix tell visitors their data is safe. Studies show SSL increases conversion rates by up to 18% in e-commerce.

PCI-DSS Compliance

Any website that accepts payment card data must have a valid SSL certificate — it's a mandatory requirement of the PCI-DSS security standard.

256-Bit Encryption

All OrbitHost SSL certificates use 256-bit AES encryption — the same standard used by banks and government institutions worldwide.

Auto-Renewal

Never let your SSL expire accidentally. Our automated renewal system keeps your certificate current without any manual intervention required.

Expert SSL Support

Our SSL specialists will help you choose the right certificate, complete validation, and install it on your server — guiding you through every step.
