FAQ

SSL Certificates — Common Questions

What is the difference between DV, OV, and EV SSL certificates?
Domain Validated (DV) SSL verifies only domain ownership and is issued in minutes — ideal for blogs and small sites. Organisation Validated (OV) SSL verifies your business identity. Extended Validation (EV) SSL undergoes the most rigorous legal verification and is recommended for e-commerce and financial services.
How long does SSL validation take?
DV SSL certificates are issued automatically in minutes. OV certificates from DigiCert typically take 1–3 business days. EV certificates require 3–7 business days for full legal verification. You will receive email updates throughout the process.
Does OrbitHost install the SSL certificate for me?
Yes. For hosting customers, SSL certificates are installed automatically. If you purchased a standalone certificate for an external server, our SSL specialists will provide installation instructions or install it on your behalf on request.
Will my SSL certificate renew automatically?
Let's Encrypt (DV) certificates renew automatically every 90 days. DigiCert OV and EV certificates are set up for automatic annual renewal. You will receive email reminders 30 and 7 days before expiry as a backup.
What happens if my SSL certificate expires?
If your SSL expires, visitors will see a browser security warning and typically leave your site. Search engines may also downrank the site. Our automated renewal systems and manual expiry checks ensure this never happens for OrbitHost customers.
