Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Orbit Cloud Ltd ("Orbit Cloud", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can exercise your rights under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and other applicable law.

What we collect

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number, company and billing address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Service data — domains registered, hosting services purchased, support tickets and invoices linked to your account.
  • Technical data — IP address and browser information collected in server logs for security and troubleshooting.

How we use it

  • To provision and manage the services you buy (hosting accounts, domain registrations, SSL certificates).
  • To send service notices — invoices, renewal reminders, support replies and security alerts.
  • To share the minimum required details with registrars and infrastructure partners when a service (such as a domain registration) requires it.

We never sell your personal data. You may request a copy, correction or deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team.

Terms of Service

Last updated: July 2026

Services and billing

  • Services are billed in advance on the cycle shown at checkout (monthly, annual or one-time). Invoices are payable by the due date shown.
  • Domain registrations are final once submitted to the registry and are generally non-refundable.
  • Hosting services carry a 30-day money-back guarantee unless otherwise stated on the plan page.
  • Overdue accounts may be suspended after notice; data for terminated accounts is retained for 14 days before deletion.

Your responsibilities

  • Keep your account credentials secure and your contact details current.
  • Maintain your own backups of website content in addition to any backups we provide.
  • Use the services in compliance with the Acceptable Use Policy below.

Liability

Services are provided on a 99.9% uptime target. Our total liability for any claim is limited to the fees paid for the affected service in the preceding three months. We are not liable for indirect or consequential losses.

Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: July 2026

You may not use Orbit Cloud services to:

  • Send unsolicited bulk email (spam) or operate open mail relays.
  • Host malware, phishing pages, or content that infringes copyright or other rights.
  • Attack, scan or disrupt other networks or systems, or attempt unauthorised access.
  • Host content that is unlawful in Kenya or in the jurisdiction where it is served.
  • Consume resources in a way that degrades service for other customers on shared platforms.

We may suspend services immediately for serious violations, and will otherwise give notice and an opportunity to remedy.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Our website uses a small number of cookies that are strictly necessary for it to function:

  • Session cookies — keep you signed in to the client portal and remember your domain cart.
  • Security cookies — protect forms against cross-site request forgery.

We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Blocking cookies in your browser may prevent the client portal and checkout from working.
