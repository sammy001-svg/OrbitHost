FAQ

Email Hosting — Common Questions

Can I use my own domain name for my email address?
Yes. All Orbit Cloud email plans use your custom domain. You can create addresses like name@yourdomain.com. We handle MX, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC DNS configuration to ensure your emails are delivered and not marked as spam.
What is the difference between OrbitMail and Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace?
OrbitMail is our own email platform — the most affordable option, ideal for basic business email. Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace add full productivity suites including desktop apps, cloud storage, video meetings, and collaboration tools. For teams needing more than email, Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace is the better choice.
Can I access my email on my phone or tablet?
Yes. All plans support IMAP, which syncs emails, sent items, folders, and read/unread status across all devices in real time. You can use any mail client (Apple Mail, Gmail app, Outlook, Thunderbird) or the browser-based webmail interface.
How many email addresses can I create?
OrbitMail allows unlimited email accounts under your domain. Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace are priced per user. All plans support email aliases and distribution groups at no extra cost.
How do I migrate my existing emails to Orbit Cloud?
We provide free email migration for all new customers from any provider including Gmail, cPanel mail, Outlook, and Zoho. Open a support ticket after subscribing and our migration team will transfer your emails and contacts with zero downtime.
